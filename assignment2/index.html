Handheld Windmill

Watch: How to make DIY handheld windmills:

Welcome to Interactivecrafts.com, our talented artist shows us how to create a handheld windmill using ready available materials! This will be a project that is both fun for children and adults alike!

Materials Needed

  • Sheet of foam
  • Scissor
  • Ruler
  • Pen
  • Straw
  • Pipe cleaners

Step 1

Draw and cut the foam sheet into a 12.5cm square, fold the paper diagonally in both directions.

Step 2

Poke a hole through the middle of the foam sheet and create cuts halfway from the end to the middle along the fold lines. Add 4 more holes beside the cuts on the edges of the foam sheet.

Step 3

Fold the four ends of the foam sheet inwards so that the side holes matches the center hole.

Step 4

Using the leftover pieces of foam sheets that was cut out, use a circular object (We used a cover of a glue stick) and stamp an impression, creating a circular cutout. Poke a hole through the center of the circular cutout.

Step 5

Wrap the pipe cleaner around the straw, leaving a small bit hanging out. Slide a foam ring onto the pipe cleaner, push the windmill through the pipe cleaner and cover the other end with a foam ring. Cut off excess pipe cleaner and twist it around the front ring.

And there you have it...

Congratulations! You've successfully made a handheld windmill! It might not spin smoothly initially, however, with a little tweaking, it will turn smoothly. Try making a few in different colours!
